Maren & Berne's Birthday Wish

Birthday Card

  • Print Design
  • Art Direction

Maren and Berne's first birthday was fast approaching and their parents wanted to do something a little different. The twins were born with Cystic Fibrosis and it was their parents dream to one day find a cure. The birthday card helped to educate people on Cystic Fibrosis and what we can do to help.
