My role: web design, HTML / CSS

Crock-pot

Crock-Pot is "The Original Slow Cooker." Since everyone has fond memories of delicious meals cooked with a Crock-Pot, the client wanted to collect and share that wealth of information with other enthusiasts. We launched Ratings & Reviews and two Stories campaigns on their website and the submissions came pouring in. Users were able to leave feedback on specific products, submit their own favorite recipes, and share a story about their most memorable moments.

HTC

HTC was the largest client in Bazaarvoice history and has some of the highest rated smart phones on the market. We rolled out Ratings & Reviews on the US and world wide sites that included over 15 different locales. It was important to clearly showcase what reviewer sentiment was on each product. Content was easily scannable and blends in seamlessly with the rest of the HTC website.
