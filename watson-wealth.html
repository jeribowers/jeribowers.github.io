The Watson for Wealth Management demo was created to show how Watson could be utilized to deepen the relationship between a Wealth Manager and his clients. The demo relied heavily on the WEX framework and also incorporated Analytics and Q&A capabilities.

Watson acts as the Wealth Managers cognitive assistant and enables him to communicate more effectively with his client using evidence based insights. Watson is able to make personalized recommendations based on the clients current portfolio, financial data, past activity, and market trends.
