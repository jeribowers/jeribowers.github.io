Austin Asian Film Festival

Showcasing the best in Asian films and cinema

My role: art direction, print design

The AAFF mission is to spread appreciation and awareness for Asian and Asian-American films and culture in the Austin commmunity. A variety of movies are showcased, from short-films and documentaries to full-length feature films. The AAFF brochure included sponsorship information, a short synopsis on each film, director information and schedule.
