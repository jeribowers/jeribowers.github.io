Watson Wealth Management

Cognitive assistant for wealth managers

My role: visual design, HTML/CSS

The Watson Wealth Management demo was created to showcase how Watson could be utilized to deepen the relationship between a Wealth Manager and his clients. The demo relied heavily on the Watson Experience Manager framework and also incorporated Watson Analytics and Q&A capabilities.

Watson was the Wealth Manager's cognitive assistant and enabled him to communicate more effectively with his client using evidence based insights. Watson was able to make personalized recommendations based on the clients current portfolio, financial data, past activity, and market trends. Financial considerations that Watson recommended were presented with it’s confidence, supporting evidence and overall sentiment.

BEFORE the design refresh...

The demo was requested for the grand opening of the new IBM office in New York. There was already an existing demo but it needed to be updated. The current design felt dated and didn't convey the confidence that a trusted advisor should.

AFTER the design refresh

I worked closely with a small group of engineers, a financial advisor and designer on the redesign. Within a few short weeks, we were able to determine the content hiearchy, give the visual design a major overhaul and have the demo ready for the official opening.
